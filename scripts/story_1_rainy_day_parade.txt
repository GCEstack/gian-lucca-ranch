The Rainy Day Parade

A Gian Lucca Ranch Story — Written with Love by Daddy

One morning, Benny the Bear woke up to a very surprising sound. 

Plip, plop, plip, plop. Rain was falling on the roof of the old oak tree! Benny peeked out his window and frowned. He had planned a big picnic with the Chicken Crew and Tommy Turkey. The sun was hiding, the sky was gray, and big raindrops were splashing everywhere.

Benny sighed a big bear sigh. "What will we do now?" he wondered.

Just then, there was a gentle knock at his door. It was Clucky, the leader of the Chicken Crew. She was wearing the silliest hat Benny had ever seen — it was an old flower pot turned upside down!

"Why the long face, Benny?" Clucky asked. "Rain is just the sky giving the ranch a nice drink of water!"

"But our picnic..." Benny said sadly.

Tommy Turkey appeared next, shaking water from his colorful feathers. "A little rain never hurt anyone!" he gobbled. "Besides, I have an idea. Follow me!"

The friends walked through the wet grass to the big red barn. Inside, they found a pile of old umbrellas, colorful scarves, and a big drum that Daddy used on special days.

Tommy Turkey spread his wings wide. "We are NOT going to let a little rain ruin our fun! Today, we are having the very first... Rainy Day Parade!"

Benny's eyes grew big. The Chicken Crew started clucking with excitement. Clucky found a bright yellow umbrella and twirled it like a dancing umbrella. Tommy Turkey wrapped himself in a blue scarf and started tapping the drum with his beak. Boom-boom, tap-tap!

Benny found a red raincoat that was much too big for him, but that made it even funnier. He marched through the barn, stomping his paws in big puddles. Splash! Splash! The water went everywhere, and everyone laughed.

Then Benny had an idea. "What if we march all the way through the meadow?"

The friends looked at each other. Then they looked at the rain. Then they all shouted, "YES!"

Outside they went, marching through the rain. The Chicken Crew held tiny umbrellas made of big leaves. Tommy Turkey played his drum. Benny splashed in every puddle he could find. They marched past the creek, past the cornfield, and all the way to the garden gate.

As they marched, something wonderful happened. The rain began to slow down. The clouds opened up, and a beautiful rainbow appeared across the sky. Red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and purple — all the colors were smiling down at the ranch.

Benny looked up at the rainbow and smiled. "You know what?" he said. "This was even better than a picnic."

Clucky nodded. "Sometimes the best adventures happen when our plans change."

Tommy Turkey fluffed his feathers proudly. "And that is why we always say... every day is a good day for a parade!"

The friends marched back to the warm barn, where Daddy had hot honey tea waiting. They sat together, listening to the soft rain, and Benny thought to himself that rainy days might just be his favorite days of all.

The end.
